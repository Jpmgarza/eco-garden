1. Information we collect

Information you provide

When you submit our contact or assessment-request form, we collect the information you choose to give us:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • City or neighborhood
  • Approximate lawn size
  • Any message or details you include

You are not required to provide information beyond the fields marked as required, but doing so helps us respond accurately.

Information collected automatically

When you visit the website, our analytics provider collects limited, aggregated, and non-identifying usage information such as pages viewed, general device and browser type, and approximate region. This information is collected without cookies and is not used to personally identify you. Our font provider may also receive your device’s IP address as a standard part of delivering web fonts.

2. How we use your information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and questions;
  • Schedule consultations and yard assessments;
  • Prepare quotes and provide our services;
  • Communicate with you about a project you have requested; and
  • Maintain records and improve our website and services.

We do not use your information for third-party advertising, and we do not sell or rent your personal information.

3. How we share your information

We share personal information only with the trusted service providers that help us operate, and only as needed for them to perform their functions:

  • Resend — delivers your form submissions to our business email inbox.
  • Vercel — hosts our website and provides privacy-friendly, cookieless analytics.
  • Google Fonts — delivers the fonts used on our website.

We may also disclose information if required to do so by law, or to protect our rights, safety, or property. We do not sell your personal information and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising.

4. Cookies and analytics

Our website does not use advertising or tracking cookies. Our analytics are cookieless and collect only aggregated, non-identifying usage data to help us understand how the site is used. Because we do not use tracking cookies, there is no advertising profile built from your visit.

5. Data retention

We keep the information you submit for as long as needed to respond to your inquiry, provide services you have requested, and maintain ordinary business records, after which we delete or anonymize it. You may ask us to delete your information at any time (see Your Colorado privacy rights).

6. Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you provide. However, no method of transmission or storage over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Colorado privacy rights

If you are a Colorado resident, the Colorado Privacy Act (CPA) gives you the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you;
  • Correct inaccuracies in your personal data;
  • Delete your personal data;
  • Obtain a portable copy of your personal data; and
  • Opt out of the sale of personal data, targeted advertising, and certain profiling (note: we do not engage in these activities).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at Jimisbuilds@gmail.com. We will respond as required by law. If we decline your request, you may appeal that decision by replying to our response or contacting us at the same address.

8. Children’s privacy

Our website and services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Third-party links

Our website may link to third-party sites or services that we do not control. This Privacy Policy does not apply to those sites, and we encourage you to review their privacy practices.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above. Material changes will be reflected on this page.
