What Is an Eco Garden?

Traditional lawns are a relic of a wetter era. They demand constant watering, weekly mowing, and a revolving door of fertilizers and pest treatments, all while draining your wallet and your weekends.

An eco garden replaces your high-maintenance turf with native Colorado plants, rich mulch beds, natural rock features, and precision drip irrigation. These systems are designed to thrive in Colorado’s climate, not fight it.

The result: a yard that turns heads on your street, uses 50–70% less water, and practically takes care of itself.

Saves Water

Native plants thrive on Colorado’s natural rainfall — no irrigation sprinklers running daily during a drought.

Low Maintenance

No mowing, no fertilizer, no stress. Once established, your eco garden largely manages itself season after season.

Increases Home Value

Modern, clean curb appeal that buyers love. A thoughtful landscape adds meaningful value to your property.

Common Questions

How long does installation take?

Most yards are fully installed in 2–5 days depending on size and complexity. We work efficiently so your home isn’t disrupted for long.

Will my HOA approve a native plant yard?

Most Colorado HOAs now permit or actively encourage native landscaping. We can help you understand the approval process and provide documentation if needed.

How much water will I actually save?

Most clients see a 60–70% reduction in outdoor water use within the first season. Native plants are adapted to Colorado’s climate and thrive on natural rainfall once established.

Do native plants need a lot of maintenance?

Much less than a traditional lawn. No mowing, minimal watering once established, and light seasonal trimming once a year is typically all that’s needed.

What happens to my existing lawn during conversion?

We remove the old grass, prepare and amend the soil for native plants, and install your new design from scratch. We handle everything start to finish.

Is the garden safe for kids and pets?

Yes. All plants we use are non-toxic, pet-safe native Colorado species. We can also design around play areas or specific zones in your yard.

What’s included in the free assessment?

A site visit, soil evaluation, a walkthrough of what’s possible for your specific yard, plant recommendations, and a rough cost estimate — all at no charge.

Do you offer any guarantee on your work?

Yes. We stand behind every installation with a plant establishment guarantee. If something doesn’t take root, we make it right.
