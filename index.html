What Is an Eco Garden?

Traditional lawns are a relic of a wetter era. They demand constant watering, weekly mowing, and a revolving door of fertilizers and pest treatments, all while draining your wallet and your weekends.

An eco garden replaces your high-maintenance turf with native Colorado plants, rich mulch beds, natural rock features, and precision drip irrigation. These systems are designed to thrive in Colorado’s climate, not fight it.

The result: a yard that turns heads on your street, uses 50–70% less water, and practically takes care of itself.

Saves Water

Native plants thrive on Colorado’s natural rainfall — no irrigation sprinklers running daily during a drought.

Low Maintenance

No mowing, no fertilizer, no stress. Once established, your eco garden largely manages itself season after season.

Increases Home Value

Modern, clean curb appeal that buyers love. A thoughtful landscape adds meaningful value to your property.
